Credits
This game, including the user interface, stage design and Spell Card system, is inspired by ZUN's Touhou Project franchise. The single in-game stage (Calendar Stage) is modelled after a Touhou-style Extra Stage.
Ordinal Project takes its name from the OMCCDV community, which itself takes its name (but is not otherwise associated with) PatcailMemer's Ordinal Markup.
Enemy & Character Art
Luigin's unused portrait is based on Jun Rhodes-Rose.
Lexan's art is based on Ji.
All other art is original.
Backgrounds
All backgrounds are either copyright-free stock images or original art.
